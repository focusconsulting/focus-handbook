# Conduct & Ethics

_Professional standards, harassment prevention, workplace safety, and ethical obligations._

## Professional Conduct & Ethics

All Focus staff must maintain the highest ethical standards, acting with honesty, integrity, and fairness in all interactions with clients, colleagues, and the public.

The following conduct is prohibited and may result in disciplinary action up to and including termination:

- Dishonesty or falsification of records
- Possession of illegal drugs or weapons
- Fighting, threats of violence, or intimidation
- Vulgar or abusive language
- Insubordination or refusal to carry out reasonable work assignments
- Unauthorized use of company equipment or property
- Excessive absence or tardiness without notification
- Harassment, discrimination, or bullying
- Unauthorized disclosure of confidential information
- Any violation of company policies

## Non-Harassment / Non-Discrimination Policy

Focus prohibits all forms of discrimination and harassment based on protected characteristics including race, sex, pregnancy, gender identity, sexual orientation, color, religion, national origin, age, disability, genetic information, and military/veteran status.

### Prohibited Conduct

- Derogatory remarks, slurs, or stereotyping
- Unwelcome physical contact or touching
- Display of offensive images or materials
- Stalking or intimidation
- Jokes or comments targeting protected characteristics

### Sexual Harassment

Sexual harassment includes unwelcome sexual advances, requests for sexual favors, and any verbal or physical conduct of a sexual nature when:

- Submission is made a condition of employment
- Submission or rejection affects employment decisions
- The conduct creates a hostile, intimidating, or offensive work environment

> **Reporting:** If you experience or witness harassment or discrimination, report it immediately to your manager or People Operations. Focus investigates all allegations promptly and prohibits any form of retaliation against individuals who report concerns in good faith.

## Workplace Violence Prevention

Focus has zero tolerance for violence, threats of violence, threatening language, or aggressive behavior in the workplace — whether in person, remote, or at client sites.

All staff must immediately report:

- Direct threats or threatening statements
- Suspicious or concerning behavior
- Any acts of physical violence
- Possession of weapons on company or client premises

The company will investigate all reports thoroughly while protecting the reporter's identity to the extent possible.

## Drug-Free Workplace

Focus maintains a drug-free workplace. The possession, manufacture, use, or distribution of controlled substances in the workplace is strictly prohibited. The company will assist employees who voluntarily seek treatment for substance addiction.

## Conflict of Interest

All staff must disclose personal, financial, or other interests that could potentially conflict with their professional responsibilities. Conflicts of interest include:

- Financial interests in clients, vendors, or competitors
- Personal relationships with clients or vendors that could influence professional judgment
- Outside employment or consulting that competes with Focus
- Accepting gifts, favors, or benefits from outside parties that could influence decision-making

The company will determine appropriate mitigation measures, which may include recusal from specific projects. Failure to disclose conflicts may result in disciplinary action.

## Confidential Company & Client Information

All client information — including personal data, financial details, and project information — must remain confidential. Access to client data is restricted to authorized personnel only.

Company confidential information includes customer lists, price lists, financial data, marketing plans, business strategies, and sensitive employee information. Employees must protect this information and may face termination for improper disclosure.

Inadvertent disclosure must be reported immediately to your manager and People Operations.

## Health & Safety

Focus maintains reasonable safety precautions for all work environments. All staff are expected to:

- Help prevent accidents and unsafe conditions
- Report all work-related injuries, regardless of severity, immediately to management
- Report any physical discomfort from repetitive tasks
- Know emergency procedures (dial 911 for emergencies)
- Maintain a safe home office environment when working remotely

## No Solicitation / No Distribution

Employees may not solicit for causes or distribute non-work materials during working time. This includes in-person solicitation, email campaigns, and use of company communication channels for non-business purposes.
