Time Off & Leave

PTO, sick leave, holidays, and federal and state leave entitlements.

Paid Time Off (PTO)

  • 15 days (120 hours) per year for regular full-time employees
  • Vacation requests require manager approval based on project needs
  • Unused PTO does not carry over to the next year, except where required by state law
  • Employees must use accrued time before requesting advances on future accruals
State Carryover Requirements
Some states require PTO carryover up to a maximum threshold. Check the State Policies section for your specific state's requirements.

Sick Leave

  • Focus provides unlimited sick leave for employees
  • Use sick leave for personal illness, medical appointments, or caring for an ill family member
  • Documentation (doctor's note) is required after 3 or more consecutive sick days
  • Additional state-specific sick leave requirements may apply — see State Policies

Family & Medical Leave (FMLA)

Eligible employees may take up to 12 weeks of unpaid leave within a 12-month period under the Family and Medical Leave Act. Eligibility requires:

  • 12 months of employment with Focus
  • At least 1,250 hours worked in the previous 12 months
  • Work at a location where Focus employs 50 or more employees within 75 miles

Qualifying Reasons

  • Birth of a child and newborn care
  • Placement of a child for adoption or foster care
  • Care for a spouse, child, or parent with a serious health condition
  • Employee's own serious health condition
  • Qualifying military exigencies
  • Care for a covered servicemember with a serious injury or illness (up to 26 weeks)

Source: Family and Medical Leave Act, 29 U.S.C. §§ 2601–2654; 29 C.F.R. Part 825

Military Leave

Employees who are members of the uniformed services may take unpaid military leave in accordance with the Uniformed Services Employment and Reemployment Rights Act (USERRA) and applicable state laws. Employees may use accrued PTO during military service at their discretion.

Source: USERRA, 38 U.S.C. §§ 4301–4335

Jury Duty

Employees summoned for jury service should provide a copy of the summons to their manager and People Operations as soon as possible. Focus will not retaliate against employees for fulfilling their civic duty.

Unpaid Leave of Absence

Employees may request temporary unpaid leave for personal, medical, or other reasons. Requests must be submitted to your manager and People Operations in advance. Approval is at the company's discretion based on business needs and the circumstances of the request.

State-Specific Leave
Many states where Focus employees are located have additional leave requirements (paid family leave, parental leave, voting leave, etc.). See the State Policies section for details specific to your state.
