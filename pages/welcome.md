# Welcome to Focus Consulting

_We're glad you're here. This handbook is your guide to working at Focus — our culture, policies, and what we expect of each other._

## About This Handbook

This handbook provides an overview of Focus Consulting's policies, practices, programs, and benefits. It is designed as an informational guide for all staff — both W2 employees and project contractors — though certain sections apply specifically to one group or the other, as marked.

> **Important:** This handbook is not a binding contract between the company and its staff. Focus retains the right to modify any provision at any time. Employees will be notified of significant changes.

## Our Mission

Build modern digital services that reduce friction, simplify complex policy, and can be sustained by the agency.

We focus on projects that drive meaningful impact. Success means delivering efficient services that improve access to critical programs, reduce the burden on users and agency staff, improve health outcomes, and streamline access to benefits.

## Employment-At-Will

Employment at Focus is at-will, meaning either the employee or the company may terminate the employment relationship at any time, with or without cause or notice. Only the President of Focus Consulting may authorize any written agreement providing different terms of employment.

## Equal Employment Opportunity

Focus Consulting is committed to providing equal employment opportunities to all employees and applicants. We prohibit discrimination based on race, sex, pregnancy, gender identity, sexual orientation, color, religion, national origin, age, disability, genetic information, military status, or any other characteristic protected by applicable law.

Focus is committed to complying with the Americans with Disabilities Act (ADA) and providing reasonable accommodations to qualified individuals with disabilities.

## Open Door Policy

Focus maintains an open door policy. If you experience workplace concerns, you are encouraged to escalate issues through your manager or People Operations without fear of retaliation. We believe open communication is essential to a productive work environment.
