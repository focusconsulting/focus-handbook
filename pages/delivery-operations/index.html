Delivery Operations

Operating procedures for Focus staff working on client engagements, including subcontract and prime contract programs.

All Staff

This section covers the processes, standards, and escalation paths that govern how Focus manages active delivery engagements. The procedures here are primarily aimed at Delivery Leads, program staff, and the contracts team, but are available to all staff for reference.

Content in this section will expand over time to cover Focus-prime and joint venture engagements. The current pages are scoped to Nava subcontracts.

Subcontract Ops

Standard operating procedures for Focus engagements where Focus serves as a subcontractor to Nava as the prime. These SOPs address the three categories of issues most likely to create delivery risk or relationship friction on a Nava subcontract.

All Staff
On-Program Escalations
How to identify, route, and resolve delivery concerns, personnel issues, and client complaints on a Nava subcontract. Includes escalation matrix and communication rules.

Staffing Transitions & KP Notifications
Notification windows and process steps when a Focus team member transitions off a Nava program, including key personnel designation and involuntary removal procedures.
