Note
This section provides a summary of Focus's benefits. Formal plan documents govern in cases of inconsistency between this summary and plan terms. Contact People Operations for detailed plan information.

Eligibility

Full-time W2 employees become eligible for most benefits on the first of the month following their hire date. Life and disability insurance eligibility begins the first of the month following 30 days of employment. Contractors are not eligible for company benefits.

Health Insurance

Focus provides health insurance through CareFirst (BCBS) via DC Health Link.

New for 2026–2027: Dependent Premium Contribution
Focus now contributes 50% of spouse and dependent health insurance premiums. Previously, Focus covered employee premiums only. This is in addition to the existing 70% contribution toward employee premiums.
ContributionFocus pays
Employee premium70%
Spouse / dependent premiums50%
  • 59 plans available for employees in the MD/DC/Northern VA area
  • 23 plans available for employees outside the local area
  • Rates are age-banded — log into your DC Health Link portal to see your personal cost
  • Already enrolled and keeping your plan? No action needed — coverage auto-renews July 1

Retirement Plan (401k)

Focus offers a 401(k) through Human Interest.

  • Auto-enrolled at 3% salary deferral starting on your hire date
  • You can adjust your contribution rate or opt out at any time through Human Interest
  • No employer match at this time

Flexible Spending Accounts

Both FSA and DCAP are administered through Further. Focus covers all administrative costs. Neither account auto-renews — you must re-enroll each benefit year.

Healthcare FSA

  • Contribute up to $3,400 per year (pre-tax via payroll)
  • Up to $640 rolls over to the next benefit year; remainder is forfeited
  • Use for eligible medical, dental, and vision expenses
  • Eligible from the 1st of the month following your hire date

Dependent Care Assistance Program (DCAP)

  • Contribute up to $7,500 per household per year (pre-tax via payroll)
  • No rollover permitted — unused funds are forfeited at year end
  • Use for eligible dependent care expenses (daycare, after-school care, elder care)
  • Eligible from the 1st of the month following your hire date

Professional Development

Focus invests in your growth with a $1,000 per year upskilling stipend. This can be used for:

  • Continuing education courses and certifications
  • Conference attendance
  • Books, online courses, and learning platforms
  • Other upskilling activities approved by your manager
