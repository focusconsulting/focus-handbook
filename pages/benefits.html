Benefits

Health insurance, retirement, professional development, and other benefits available to full-time employees.

Note
This section provides a summary of Focus's benefits. Formal plan documents govern in cases of inconsistency between this summary and plan terms. Contact People Operations for detailed plan information.

Eligibility

Full-time W2 employees become eligible for benefits on the first of the month following their hire date. Contractors are not eligible for company benefits.

Health Insurance

Focus provides health insurance through CareFirst via DC Health Link.

  • 59 plans available for employees in the MD/DC/Northern VA area
  • 23 plans available for employees outside the local area (national plans)
  • Coverage begins the 1st of the month following your hire date

Contact People Operations for the full list of available plans based on your location.

Dental Insurance

Dental coverage is provided through Beam Dental.

Coverage TierEmployee Cost (after Focus contribution)
Employee Only$21.26/month
Employee + FamilyUp to $60.67/month

Plan highlights:

  • $0 deductible for preventive services
  • $50 individual deductible for other care
  • PPO plan with in-network and out-of-network coverage

Vision Insurance

Vision coverage is provided through Beam Vision.

Coverage TierEmployee Cost
Employee Only$4.28/month
Employee + FamilyUp to $14.41/month

Plan highlights:

  • $10 copay for eye exams
  • $10 copay for materials (lenses and frames)

Life & Disability Insurance

Focus provides the following coverage at no cost to employees, through Principal:

BenefitCoverage
Life Insurance / AD&D$200,000 benefit
Short-Term Disability60% of pre-disability earnings
Long-Term Disability60% of pre-disability earnings

Retirement Plan (401k)

  • Auto-enrollment at 3% of salary (you may adjust or opt out)
  • Both pre-tax and post-tax (Roth) contribution options available
  • No employer match at this time
  • Eligible after 90 days of employment

Flexible Spending Accounts

Healthcare FSA

  • Contribute up to $3,200 per year (pre-tax)
  • Up to $640 rolls over to the next benefit year
  • Use for eligible medical, dental, and vision expenses

Dependent Care Assistance Program (DCAP)

  • Contribute up to $5,000 per year (pre-tax)
  • Use for eligible dependent care expenses (daycare, after-school care, elder care)

Professional Development

Focus invests in your growth with a $1,000 per year professional development stipend. This can be used for:

  • Continuing education courses
  • Professional certifications
  • Conference attendance
  • Books, online courses, and learning platforms
  • Other upskilling activities approved by your manager
