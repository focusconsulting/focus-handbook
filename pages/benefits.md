# Benefits

_Health insurance, retirement, professional development, and other benefits available to full-time employees. Plan year: July 1, 2026 – June 30, 2027._

> **Note:** This section provides a summary of Focus's benefits. Formal plan documents govern in cases of inconsistency between this summary and plan terms. Contact People Operations for detailed plan information.

## Eligibility

Full-time W2 employees become eligible for most benefits on the **first of the month following their hire date**. Life and disability insurance eligibility begins the first of the month following 30 days of employment. Contractors are not eligible for company benefits.

## Health Insurance

Focus provides health insurance through **[CareFirst (BCBS) via DC Health Link](https://www.dchealthlink.com/)**.

> **New for 2026–2027: Dependent Premium Contribution:** Focus now contributes 50% of spouse and dependent health insurance premiums . Previously, Focus covered employee premiums only. This is in addition to the existing 70% contribution toward employee premiums.

| Contribution | Focus pays |
| --- | --- |
| Employee premium | 70% |
| Spouse / dependent premiums | 50% |

- **59 plans** available for employees in the MD/DC/Northern VA area
- **23 plans** available for employees outside the local area
- Rates are age-banded — log into your [DC Health Link portal](https://www.dchealthlink.com/) to see your personal cost
- Already enrolled and keeping your plan? No action needed — coverage auto-renews July 1

## Dental Insurance

Dental coverage is provided through **[Beam Benefits](https://www.beambenefits.com/dental)**. Focus contributes 50% of the monthly premium.

| Coverage Tier | Your cost / month |
| --- | --- |
| Employee only | $23.12 |
| Employee + Spouse | $46.23 |
| Employee + Child(ren) | $42.86 |
| Family | $65.98 |

Plan highlights:

- $0 deductible for preventive services (exams, cleanings, fluoride, x-rays)
- $50 individual / $150 family deductible for basic and major services
- PPO plan with in-network and out-of-network coverage

## Vision Insurance

Vision coverage is provided through **[Beam Benefits](https://www.beambenefits.com/vision)**. Focus contributes 50% of the monthly premium.

| Coverage Tier | Your cost / month |
| --- | --- |
| Employee only | $4.53 |
| Employee + Spouse | $9.05 |
| Employee + Child(ren) | $9.70 |
| Family | $15.28 |

Plan highlights:

- $10 copay for eye exams; $10 copay for materials
- $150 frame or contact lens allowance per year
- 20% savings on additional prescription and non-prescription eyewear
- ~15% average discount on laser vision correction

## Life & Disability Insurance

Focus provides the following coverage at **no cost to employees** through **Principal**. You are auto-enrolled — no action required.

| Benefit | Coverage | Eligibility |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Life Insurance / AD&D | $200,000 benefit | 1st of month following 30 days of employment |
| Short-Term Disability | 60% of pre-disability earnings, up to 13 weeks | 1st of month following 30 days of employment |
| Long-Term Disability | 60% of pre-disability monthly earnings | 1st of month following 30 days of employment |

If you are unable to work for more than 8 consecutive days, contact People Operations to initiate a Short-Term Disability claim.

## Retirement Plan (401k)

Focus offers a 401(k) through **[Human Interest](https://humaninterest.com/)**.

- Auto-enrolled at **3% salary deferral** starting on your hire date
- You can adjust your contribution rate or opt out at any time through Human Interest
- No employer match at this time

## Flexible Spending Accounts

Both FSA and DCAP are administered through **[Further](https://hellofurther.com/)**. Focus covers all administrative costs. **Neither account auto-renews — you must re-enroll each benefit year.**

### Healthcare FSA

- Contribute up to **$3,400 per year** (pre-tax via payroll)
- Up to **$640** rolls over to the next benefit year; remainder is forfeited
- Use for eligible medical, dental, and vision expenses
- Eligible from the 1st of the month following your hire date

### Dependent Care Assistance Program (DCAP)

- Contribute up to **$7,500 per household per year** (pre-tax via payroll)
- No rollover permitted — unused funds are forfeited at year end
- Use for eligible dependent care expenses (daycare, after-school care, elder care)
- Eligible from the 1st of the month following your hire date

## Professional Development

Focus invests in your growth with a **$1,000 per year** upskilling stipend. This can be used for:

- Continuing education courses and certifications
- Conference attendance
- Books, online courses, and learning platforms
- Other upskilling activities approved by your manager
